MIND BEFORE SIGHT


by: Maclin



TYPE


This is where I type everything!



Hiii


Abstract Art


Making abstract art is easy…

The hard part,

Is doing it.



Walking on Sunshine


There is light beneath my feet

I don’t look down for fear of being blinded.


The horizon is bright enough

I’ll keep my eyes focused there

Until I make it home

For a good nights rest.




I Wonder


I wonder what they’ll say when they read this!

It’s a GitHub page

Linked to an html file

Literally a typewriter that prints to the internet.

LOLOLOL!!




Listen


I’m literally just going to type

All day and all night

To bring these words

From my brain

To your homepage
