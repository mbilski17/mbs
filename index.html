ECLIPSE


Hide away

Hide your face

I’ll see you soon enough


Your space

Your grace

Veiled by night


I wait

I wait

Eager to receive your light


Goodbye what was

Hello what is


Ash to dust

Water to mist


Here is the rain,

it will clear the sky

and reveal the all the answers

I desire to know.
